Data Science and Cloud-Native Development


Lebanon Crisis Analytics Team Data Analysis Hub (LCAT-DAH)

2022-2023, LCAT, Mercy Corps

  • Live Site

The Lebanon Crisis Analytics Team Data Analysis Hub is a groundbreaking platform that provides critical insights into the economic situation in Lebanon. Our team led the comprehensive, multifaceted full-stack project that spanned eight months in the initial push and has had two additional task orders, building a chained machine learning model to forecast exchange rates and prices for essential items such as food, fuel, and consumer price indices.

We also designed and implemented custom visualizations to analyze the model's performance, generate price forecasts, project pass-through rates, and map the Economic Vulnerability Index. Furthermore, we implemented web analytics tracking using Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to enable greater understanding of user behavior within the application.

We are proud to have played, and continue to play a large role, the development of this platform, which provides essential insights into the economic challenges facing Lebanon and informs critical decision-making for international aid organizations.


Horn of Africa Livestock Hub

2022-2023, Data For Impact (D4I), Mercy Corps

Built to support Mercy Corps' strategic imperatives related to regional food insecurity in the Horn of Africa, the Horn of Africa Livestock Hub showcases interactive data visualizations for environmental factors, livestock production, trade, and rangeland health. It also features a Livestock System Dynamics Model that helps to understand the impact of various factors, including environmental and social, on food security in the region.

The model allows fordata-driven insights into the livestock system dynamics, providing decision-makers with information to better manage food systems in the region. Argot Nova provided end-to-end services for this project, including building data pipelines, full-stack development, and hosting the application. Overall, this project showcases the potential impact of technology and data analysis in planning for sustainable and resilient food systems as well as highlights the importance of data-driven solutions in addressing complex societal challenges, such as food insecurity.


Middle East Regional Hub (MERDAH)

2023 (ongoing), Middle East Regional Crisis Analysis Team, Mercy Corps

  • Live Site

Forming the initial foundation for a multi-purpose and comprehensive data hub for Mercy Corps' Middle East Crisis Analysis Team, MERDAH combines GDP inference, food price forecasts, inflation-monitoring, consumer price index forecasts, and affordability with local wages analyses. Linking the analytical efforts of multiple teams, Argot Nova built the data architecture, pipelines, forecasts, web analytics monitoring, web application to support this ambitious regional effort and will continue to add utility in future task orders.


Yemen Markets and Economics Platform

2022-2023

The Yemen Markets and Economics Platform is an interactive data visualization tool designed to help organizations and individuals better understand the economic situation in Yemen. The platform provides users with an easy-to-use interface that allows them to explore economic trends and indicators across the country. It hosts historical data and forecasts for a range of economic indicators, including inflation rates, currency exchange rates, food prices, fuel prices, and wages, all of which can be viewed at various levels of granularity, from the national to the local level. This dashboard's public affiliation is restricted due to regional sensitivities about predictive analytics in Yemen.

The platform features custom food price forecast models which were built by Argot Nova using model chaining. Additionally, to enable analytics post launch, Argot Nova integrated Google Analytics to collect and monitor user behavior, including most-visited pages, scrolling, clicking, and selecting.
